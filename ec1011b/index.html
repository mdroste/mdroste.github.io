Section Materials for Economics 1011B: Intermediate Macroeconomics (advanced)

These are the section slides I wrote as a teaching fellow for Harvard’s Economics 1011B (intermediate macroeconomics) in Spring 2023. These slides roughly follow Pablo Kurlat’s textbook and lecture materials by Ludwig Straub (first half) and Gabriel Chodorow-Reich (second half).
