Ec2450A Materials

Section Materials for Economics 2450A: Public Economics and Fiscal Policy I

These are the section slides I wrote as a teaching fellow for Harvard’s Economics 2450A, the first half of Harvard’s two-semester graduate public economics sequence, in Fall 2022. This course was taught by Stefanie Stantcheva, and notably is focused heavily on optimal tax theory.

These section slides closely follow along with Stefanie Stantcheva’s lecture slides, which she has kindly posted online here.
